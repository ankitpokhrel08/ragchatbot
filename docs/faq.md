# Sagarmatha Bank - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Account Opening

### Q1. How can I open a bank account?

You can open an account by visiting any branch with valid identification documents or through digital onboarding if available.

### Q2. What documents are required for account opening?

A citizenship certificate, passport, driving license, or other government-issued identification document is required.

### Q3. Can minors open bank accounts?

Yes. Minor accounts can be opened through a parent or legal guardian.

### Q4. What is KYC?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is the verification process required before account activation.

### Q5. How long does account opening take?

Most accounts are activated within the same business day after verification.

---

## Savings Accounts

### Q6. What is the minimum balance requirement?

Minimum balance requirements depend on the account type.

### Q7. How is interest calculated?

Interest is generally calculated on the daily closing balance and credited periodically.

### Q8. Can I have multiple savings accounts?

Yes, customers may maintain multiple accounts subject to bank policies.

### Q9. When is interest credited?

Interest is credited according to the bank's published schedule.

### Q10. Can I convert my account type later?

Yes, subject to eligibility and documentation requirements.

---

## Fixed Deposits

### Q11. What is a Fixed Deposit (FD)?

An FD is a deposit maintained for a fixed period at a predetermined interest rate.

### Q12. What is the minimum FD amount?

The minimum amount depends on the product selected.

### Q13. Can I withdraw an FD before maturity?

Yes, but premature withdrawal penalties may apply.

### Q14. Can I renew an FD automatically?

Yes, auto-renewal options are available.

### Q15. Can I use my FD as loan collateral?

Yes, FDs may be pledged as collateral subject to approval.

### Q16. What types of loans are available?

Home loans, personal loans, auto loans, education loans, agricultural loans, and business loans.

### Q17. How is loan eligibility determined?

Eligibility is based on income, credit history, repayment capacity, and collateral.

### Q18. How long does loan approval take?

Processing times vary depending on documentation and loan type.

### Q19. Can I repay my loan early?

Yes, early repayment is generally allowed.

### Q20. Is there a prepayment charge?

Prepayment charges may apply depending on loan terms.

An additional verification step that improves account security.
